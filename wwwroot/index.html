WASABI WALLET

The Privacy focused Bitcoin wallet

Tor Everywhere

All your traffic is routed through the Tor Network, ensuring complete privacy and anonymity. Wasabi’s custom Tor integration ensures that no two important requests appear to originate from the same source, making it virtually impossible for anyone to trace your activity. Whether you're sending transactions or synchronizing with the blockchain, your privacy is always protected.

Private Blockchain Synchronization

Wasabi uses Compact Filters to synchronize with the blockchain privately. Instead of relying on a central server or having to download the entire blockchain, Wasabi requests only the relevant blocks for your transactions. This ensures that no one can determine how much Bitcoin you own or which transactions belong to you.

Multi-Party Trasactions

Wasabi integrates the WabiSabi Trustless Multi-Party Transaction Engine, a cutting-edge protocol for collaborative transactions. This innovative feature offers several advantages:

  • Hide Payment Origins: Conceal the inputs used for payments from recipients and observers.
  • Batch Payments: Combine multiple payments into a single transaction to save on mining fees and reduce change outputs.
  • Self-Spend Consolidation: Make it difficult for senders or observers to track how your funds are used or consolidated.
Wasabi ensures trustless privacy: your security is guaranteed by cryptography, not third-party promises. Find a third-party Wasabi Coordination Service Provider and start performing multi-party transactions with Wasabi today!

Silent-Payments

Silent Payments allow you to create a single, static address for sharing with friends, accepting donations, or posting publicly without compromising privacy. Unlike traditional Bitcoin addresses, Silent Payments can be reused while ensuring that any transaction received on it remains unlinkable to other ones. Note: Wasabi currently supports sending to Silent Payment addresses.

Support for generating and receiving to Silent Payment addresses is coming soon!

Labels Management

Properly managing labels is crucial for preserving privacy. Wasabi provides a comprehensive labeling system to help you track transactions and avoid privacy leaks. By labeling coins you can ensure that funds sent by a specific entity will be prioritarily reused when sending payments back to them.

Wasabi also provides a clear list of entities that will know about a given payment, giving you full transparency and control.

Privacy Suggestions for Payments

Wasabi actively helps you understand & improve the privacy implications of your transactions. When making a payment, Wasabi provides actionable suggestions to minimize privacy risks. For example:

  • Adjusting the payment amount slightly to avoid creating a change output.
  • Warning about the entities that will be made aware that this payment is yours.
These suggestions not only protect your privacy but also improve the efficiency of your transactions, saving you money on fees.

Coin Control

For users who want granular control over their transactions, Wasabi offers advanced Coin Control features. While Wasabi’s engine automatically selects the best coins for your transactions, you can manually choose specific coins to use. This allows for precise management of your UTXOs, ensuring that the selection fits your most specific needs.

RPC

While the graphical interface might not cover very specific use cases, Wasabi provides an RPC Interface to be able to interact directly with core features of the Wallet, allowing the creation of complete scripts and integrations
